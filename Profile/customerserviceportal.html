Suggestion/Dispute
1. Delivered but not received
Please check with your neighbors or apartment office. If you still can't find it, contact customer support with your tracking number.
2. Where is my order?
You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email on the courier's website.
3. Why are some items from my order missing?
Your items may have been shipped separately. Check your shipping confirmation for multiple tracking numbers. If the tracking shows delivery for all items, please contact support.
4. What if I received a wrong item?
Contact our support team immediately with your order number and a photo of the incorrect item received. We will arrange a return and send the correct item.
5. How long will it take for my refund to be processed?
Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days after the returned items are received and inspected. The funds may take longer to appear in your account depending on your bank.
How long will the order take to arrive?
Standard shipping usually takes 7-10 business days. Expedited options are available at checkout.
Why is there no tracking update?
Tracking updates can sometimes take 24-48 hours to appear after the initial shipment email. If it persists, please contact us.
What if I received a defective item?
We apologize! Please provide photos or a video of the defect to our support team for a swift resolution (replacement or refund).
How do I return items?
Please visit our Returns Center and enter your order number and email address to start the automated return process.
How do I cancel my order?
Orders can only be canceled within 2 hours of placing them. Please contact support immediately if you need to cancel.
