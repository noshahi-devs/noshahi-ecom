General.

The NOSHAHI Points program (“NOSHAHI Points Program”) under which you may earn NOSHAHI Points operates under this Bonus Point Policy. To earn NOSHAHI Points, you must have a valid User Account.
NOSHAHI Points may only be redeemed toward the purchase of products on us.Noshahi.com (the “Site”) and the NOSHAHI US mobile application (the “App”).
NOSHAHI Points have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for cash or Wallet credit. NOSHAHI Points are void where prohibited.

NOSHAHI Points Expiration.

Each set of NOSHAHI Points has an expiration date. Points will expire if you do not redeem them by their specified expiration date. If your Points expire, you will no longer be able to redeem them.

Viewing NOSHAHI Points.

NOSHAHI Points are viewable by logging into your NOSHAHI User Account and clicking the "My Points" section under the "My Assets" section of your User Account.

Redeeming NOSHAHI Points.

NOSHAHI Points can only be redeemed towards the purchase of products on the Site or App, and not for shipping, taxes, or fees.

You can use Points to deduct up to a maximum of 50% of the total order price.

Points that expire first are used first.

NOSHAHI Points are personal and cannot be transferred to anyone.

Points may only be redeemed on the same NOSHAHI site where they were originally earned.

Order Cancellation, Returns and Refunds.

  1. If an unpaid order is cancelled, Points used will be returned.
  2. If you return items purchased using Points, Points are refunded unless expired.
  3. If you earned Points for an order and later return items, those earned Points will be deducted (may go negative until future Points bring it to zero).
Deduction of Points.

Fraudulently earned Points may be deducted at NOSHAHI’s discretion.

Termination.

NOSHAHI may terminate the Points Program anytime. When your account is closed, all Points are canceled.
